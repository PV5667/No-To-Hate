Hate Speech Online

Hate speech can affect many people as they can become mentally, emotionally, and even physically insecure. When social media users see posts, tweets, and comments, spreading hate towards them or their identity, the user's self esteem is lowered.

Hate speech and violence have a direct relationship with each other showing that when hate speech increases, hate crimes also increase. An example of this is when anti-refugee posts were shared on social media in Germany 2017, attacks on refugees also increased.

Hate speech is harmful on social media and our website categories potential statements into different categories before they are posted to social media. If the statement is hurtful and dangerous, it is categorized as such and will not be allowed to be posted. Our website uses multiple data sets and machine learning to determine hateful statements.
